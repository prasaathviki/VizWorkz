About Viz Player Pro

Enjoy smooth playback with easy controls, and Easily edit video clips and export to multiple formats.

Video Player Features

  • Smooth Playback - Fully compatible with multiple formats including: .mp4, .mpeg, .mpg, .avi, .wmv, .mkv, .3gp, .flv, .m4v, .mov, .mts, .vob, .webm
  • Essential Controls - Play, pause, next/previous navigation, volume up/down, and mute.
  • Drag & Drop - Instantly load videos by dragging them into the player.
  • Offline & Lightweight - No internet required, optimized for speed and efficiency.

Video Editor Features

  • Video Cut Out - Extract selected portions from a video, then combine and save them as a new clip.
  • Video Cut Off - Remove unwanted portions from a video, then combine the remaining portions and save them as a new clip.
  • Video Merge - Combine multiple videos into one continuous, seamless video.
  • Encoding Support - Export your videos with multiple encoding options:
    • MP4 (H.264) - Lossless, very fast, with slight timing variance.
    • MP4 (H.264 - OpenH264) - Accurate video cuts, with balanced quality.
    • WebM (VP8) - Accurate video cuts, legacy WebM format.
    • WebM (VP9) - Accurate video cuts, modern WebM format, slower encoding.
